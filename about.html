Our Story

The Journey of Energenius

How we will become leaders in energy management technology

Energenius was founded in 2024 by a group of AI Engineer students who shared a common vision: to make energy management accessible, efficient, and sustainable for everyone.

What started as a small idea as a project in system design and software engineering course will be grown into a global company serving thousands of homes and businesses around the world. Our journey has been guided by innovation, dedication, and a commitment to creating a greener future.

in future , Energenius will be at the forefront of energy management technology, combining AI, IoT, and data analytics to provide intelligent solutions that save energy, reduce costs, and minimize environmental impact.
