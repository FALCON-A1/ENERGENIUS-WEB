Product Questions

What is Energenius?

Energenius is a comprehensive smart energy management system designed to help homeowners and businesses monitor, analyze, and optimize their energy consumption. Our solution combines hardware devices with advanced software to provide real-time insights and automated energy-saving features.

How does Energenius work?

Energenius works by connecting to your electrical system through our smart energy monitor. This device tracks your energy usage in real-time and sends data to our cloud platform. Our AI-powered software analyzes this data to identify energy-saving opportunities and provides personalized recommendations. You can control and monitor everything through our mobile app or web dashboard.

What are the main features of Energenius?

  • Real-time monitoring: Track your energy usage with detailed analytics and personalized dashboards.
  • Smart automation: Automatically control devices to optimize energy consumption.
  • AI recommendations: Receive personalized suggestions to reduce energy waste.
  • Device integration: Connect with other smart home devices for a comprehensive solution.
  • Energy cost estimation: Understand the financial impact of your energy usage.
  • Carbon footprint tracking: Monitor your environmental impact in real-time.

Is Energenius compatible with other smart home devices?

Yes, Energenius is designed to integrate seamlessly with popular smart home devices and platforms, including Google Home, Amazon Alexa, Apple HomeKit, and more. Our open API also allows for custom integrations with other systems and devices.

Installation & Setup

How difficult is it to install Energenius?

Energenius is designed for easy installation. The main energy monitor can be installed by anyone with basic DIY skills in about 30 minutes using our step-by-step guide. However, for safety reasons, we recommend professional installation for the main energy monitor that connects to your electrical panel. Smart plugs and other peripheral devices can be easily installed by users without any technical expertise.

Do you offer professional installation services?

Yes, we partner with certified electricians in most regions who can professionally install your Energenius system. You can request installation services during checkout for an additional fee, which varies by location. Professional installation ensures your system is set up correctly and safely.

What do I need to get started with Energenius?

To get started with Energenius, you'll need:

  • The Energenius hardware kit (energy monitor and/or smart plugs)
  • A stable WiFi connection
  • A smartphone or tablet for the mobile app (iOS or Android)
  • Access to your electrical panel (for the main energy monitor installation)

Once you have these, you can follow our setup guide to install the hardware and configure the software.

Technical Issues

What should I do if my Energenius device is not connecting to WiFi?

If your Energenius device is not connecting to WiFi, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Ensure your WiFi network is operational and your internet connection is stable.
  2. Verify that your WiFi signal is strong enough where the device is installed. Consider using a WiFi extender if needed.
  3. Restart your WiFi router and the Energenius device.
  4. Check that you've entered the correct WiFi password during setup.
  5. Make sure your WiFi operates on the 2.4GHz band, as some of our devices don't support 5GHz networks.
  6. Reset the device to factory settings by pressing and holding the reset button for 10 seconds.

If the issue persists, please contact our technical support team for assistance.

How accurate is the energy consumption data?

Energenius energy monitors provide data with over 99% accuracy in most installations. Our devices use high-precision sensors that measure voltage, current, and power factor to calculate energy consumption. The measurements are calibrated against utility-grade meters and regularly updated through firmware updates to maintain accuracy.

Is my data secure with Energenius?

Yes, data security is a top priority at Energenius. We implement industry-standard encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your energy data is protected using TLS/SSL protocols during transmission, and stored in secure, encrypted databases. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent. Our systems undergo regular security audits and comply with relevant data protection regulations.

How often is the app and firmware updated?

We release app updates approximately once a month, introducing new features, improvements, and bug fixes. Firmware updates for the hardware devices are released quarterly or as needed to address security concerns, improve performance, or add new functionality. All updates are automatically installed to ensure you always have the latest features and security protections.
