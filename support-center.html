Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Energenius products and services

What should I do if my device won't connect to WiFi?

If your Energenius device is not connecting to WiFi, try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Ensure your WiFi network is operational and your internet connection is stable.
  2. Verify that your WiFi signal is strong enough where the device is installed. Consider using a WiFi extender if needed.
  3. Restart your WiFi router and the Energenius device.
  4. Check that you've entered the correct WiFi password during setup.
  5. Make sure your WiFi operates on the 2.4GHz band, as some of our devices don't support 5GHz networks.
  6. Reset the device to factory settings by pressing and holding the reset button for 10 seconds.

If the issue persists, please contact our technical support team for assistance.

How do I update the firmware on my devices?

Firmware updates are automatically managed through the Energenius app. However, you can manually check for updates by following these steps:

  1. Open the Energenius mobile app and log in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings > Devices.
  3. Select the device you want to update.
  4. Tap on "Device Info" and then "Check for Updates."
  5. If an update is available, tap "Update Now" and follow the on-screen instructions.

Important: Keep your device plugged in and connected to WiFi during the update process. Do not power off the device or close the app until the update is complete.

Can I use Energenius with my existing smart home system?

Yes, Energenius is designed to integrate seamlessly with most popular smart home systems. We officially support:

  • Amazon Alexa
  • Google Home
  • Apple HomeKit
  • Samsung SmartThings
  • IFTTT
  • Home Assistant

To set up an integration, go to Settings > Integrations in the Energenius app, and select your smart home platform. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the connection process.
